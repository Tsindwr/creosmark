📖 Sunder "Roll Under" Rules

The Sunder TTRPG uses a deterministic D20 "Roll Under" system where success is determined by comparing your roll to your Potential (P) and Resistance (R):

Success Level Condition Color Has Cost?
Crit Roll = P Sunder Yellow No
Success R < roll < P Green No
Mixed 1 ≤ roll ≤ R Orange (flashing) Yes
Fail P < roll < 20 Red Yes
Miff Roll = 20 Sunder Purple Yes (double)

💡 How to Use

  • Click "🎲 Roll D20" to make a skill check
  • The result will show with the appropriate color and emoji based on the success level
  • P (Potential): Your skill/stat score - higher is better
  • R (Resistance): Represents strain or damage - creates a "mixed" zone for low rolls
  • When R = 0, all rolls between 1 and P are successes (no mixed zone)
  • Success levels marked with asterisk (*) involve some cost (stress, fallout, or narrative consequences)

🎯 Examples

  • P=10, R=3: Roll 10 = Crit, Roll 7 = Success, Roll 3 = Mixed, Roll 15 = Fail
  • P=15, R=5: Roll 15 = Crit, Roll 10 = Success, Roll 4 = Mixed, Roll 18 = Fail
  • P=5, R=0: Roll 5 = Crit, Roll 3 = Success (no mixed zone), Roll 8 = Fail
  • Any config: Roll 20 = Miff (critical failure, even if P=20)
