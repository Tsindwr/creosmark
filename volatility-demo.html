About Volatility Dice & Perks

Volatility Dice represent a character's experience and control over specific actions. Each die can have perks assigned to specific slot numbers, which activate when you roll that value.

Die Sizes: D4 (2 slots) → D6 (4 slots) → D8 (6 slots) → D10 (8 slots) → D12 (10 slots)

Jinx Threshold: When stress is high and you roll low, bad things can happen! Slots in the jinx threshold (≤ stress) are highlighted in red.

Available Perks

Refresh (2 Beats) - Remove 1 Stress from this Potential's track
Implode (2 Beats) - Roll one die level below your current die size and take the result
Cleave (3 Beats) - Roll 2 dice, take the result furthest from the middle
Drive (3 Beats) - Reroll the kept die and take the resulting value
Burn (5 Beats) - Spend 1 Resistance for an automatic max Volatility value
Fracture (5 Beats) - In jinx threshold: minimum value. Outside: maximum value
