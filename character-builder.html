Click individual "Roll" buttons to roll each stat, or "Roll All 8 Stats" to roll everything at once. Drag rolled values from below onto the potentials above to assign them.

📖 How to Use

  • Roll Individual Stats: Click "Roll" button on each dice roller to generate a random value using 3d6 drop highest
  • Roll All Stats: Click "Roll All 8 Stats" to roll all potentials at once
  • Assign Stats (Drag & Drop): Drag rolled values from the dice rollers and drop them onto your desired potentials
  • Assign Stats (Click): Click a rolled value to select it (gold border), then click a potential to assign it
  • Manual Entry: You can also type values directly into the input fields (1-18)
  • Clear Assignment: Click "Clear" button to return a stat value back to the roller
  • View Sheet: Click "View Character Sheet" to see your stats displayed with potential tracks
  • Your stats are automatically saved as you make changes
