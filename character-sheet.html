📖 How to Use the Character Sheet

  • Potential Tracks: Each track shows your character's potential score with stress (red) and resistance (green)
  • Adding Stress/Resistance: Click the center circle to add stress (left-click) or resistance (right-click)
  • Modifying Nodes: Click individual nodes to increment/decrement stress or resistance
  • Keyboard Navigation: Use Tab to navigate, Enter/Space for stress, R for resistance
  • Activity Log: See a history of all changes made to your character's potentials
  • Edit Character: Click "Edit Character" to modify base stats, name, and avatar
